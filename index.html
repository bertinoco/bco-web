Content systems for teams that can't afford inconsistency.

We help ambitious scale-ups design and implement content systems — the language infrastructure and AI-assisted tooling that keep your content consistent, compliant, and clear across every touchpoint.

About

Bertino Consulting is a content systems practice led by Joe Bertino, a practitioner who designs the language infrastructure behind complex products.

For over a decade, Joe has worked embedded inside leading fintechs — including Klarna, Tink, and Trustly — designing content systems that support compliance and sustainable growth.

Today, that same approach extends beyond fintech into media and gaming. His current embedded engagement is with Mojang Studios, where he leads UX writing across the Minecraft player experience. Joe works directly in codebases and design systems — creating PRs, managing branches, and shipping alongside engineers and design system leads, not just upstream of them.

Originally from upstate New York, Joe has worked with agencies and startups across New York City, San Francisco, and Copenhagen. He's been based in Stockholm since 2014, where he lives with his wife and children.

Services

Embedded consultancy

I join your product or UX team as an embedded partner, helping you design, test, and scale language systems from the inside. This model accelerates delivery while building long-term content operations that stay clear, compliant, and consistent after I leave.

Fractional advisor

I provide ongoing strategic support for solo content designers and UX writers — acting as a senior partner to review work, set priorities, and build team confidence. This model helps growing organizations maintain quality and momentum without the overhead of a full-time hire.

Audits as a service

I conduct focused audits of your UX content and AI-assisted workflows to uncover gaps, inconsistencies, and opportunities for optimization. You'll receive actionable insights, prioritized recommendations, and a roadmap for improving clarity, compliance, and discoverability at scale.

Something else?

Get in touch to schedule a discovery call or submit a custom proposal. If I can't help, I'll do my best to refer you to someone great.

How I like to collaborate

I work best as part of your team — hybrid in Stockholm or fully remote across the EU. Engagements start at six months, billed hourly at 1 200 SEK. I'm open to occasional travel when it adds value.
