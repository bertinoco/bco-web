About

Bertino Consulting is a content systems practice led by Joe Bertino — a UX content designer who works at the edge of language, technology, and product.

For over a decade, Joe has worked embedded inside leading fintechs — including Klarna, Tink, and Trustly — designing content that supports compliance and sustainable growth. He works directly in codebases and design systems, not just upstream of them.

He currently provides UX writing leadership within the Player Experience domain at Mojang Studios (Minecraft). Increasingly, Joe works across the boundary between product and market — helping teams tell the story of what they're building with the same clarity and precision they bring to the product itself.

Originally from upstate New York, Joe has worked with agencies and startups in New York City, San Francisco, and Copenhagen. He now lives in Stockholm with his wife and children.

Services

Embedded consultancy

I join your product or UX team as an embedded partner, helping you design, test, and scale language systems from the inside. This model accelerates delivery while building long-term content operations that stay clear, compliant, and consistent after I leave.

Fractional advisor

I provide ongoing strategic support for solo content designers and UX writers — acting as a senior partner to review work, set priorities, and build team confidence. This model helps growing organizations maintain quality and momentum without the overhead of a full-time hire.

Audits as a service

I conduct focused audits of your UX content and AI-assisted workflows to uncover gaps, inconsistencies, and opportunities for optimization. You'll receive actionable insights, prioritized recommendations, and a roadmap for improving clarity, compliance, and discoverability at scale.

Something else?

Get in touch to schedule a discovery call or submit a custom proposal. If I can't help, I'll do my best to refer you to someone great.

How I like to collaborate

I usually work in a hybrid setup for Stockholm-based teams, or fully remote across the EU. My projects are based on an hourly rate of 1 200 SEK, with a minimum engagement of six months. I'm not looking to relocate, but I'm open to occasional travel when it adds value.
