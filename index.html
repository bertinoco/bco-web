About

Bertino Consulting is a content systems practice led by Joe Bertino, a UX consultant who uses language to simplify complex products.

For over a decade, Joe has worked with leading fintechs — including Klarna, Tink, and Trustly — designing content that ensures compliance and drives sustainable growth.

Today, that same approach extends beyond fintech into media and gaming. His current embedded assignment is with Mojang Studios, where he ships copy to code alongside designers and engineers from Minecraft's core UI and design system teams.

Originally from upstate New York, Joe has worked with agencies and startups across New York City, San Francisco, and Copenhagen. He's been based in Stockholm since 2014, where he lives with his wife and children.

Services

Embedded consultancy

Hire me as an embedded partner, helping you design and scale language infrastructure from the inside. This model focuses on establishing a custom content system that accelerates team decisions and operations, even after I leave.

Fractional advisor

Request support for solo content designers. Acting as a senior partner, I'll help to review work, set priorities, and build confidence. This model helps maintain quality and momentum without the overhead of a full-time hire.

Audits as a service

Get a focused audit of your content operations to uncover gaps, inconsistencies, and opportunities for improvement. You'll receive actionable insights and a roadmap you can act on.

Something else?
